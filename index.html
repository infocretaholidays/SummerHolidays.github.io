Accordion FAQ

Airport Transfers

What to expect when you arrive at Crete Heraklion Airport:

This is the second busiest airport serving Greece after Athens International Airport, so skip the queue for the local taxis and pre-book your Heraklion airport transfer to your resort with us. Once your plane has landed, you will find one of our friendly and professional drivers waiting for you in the arrivals hall, ready to drive you straight to your hotel. If your flight is delayed, your driver will know about it and collect you from Heraklion airport based on the new flight arrival time.
Where is Heraklion Airport? 
Heraklion International Airport (HER), also known as Nikos Kazantzakis airport, is the primary airport on the island of Crete, Greece, and is located 5 km to the east of Heraklion, near Nea Alikarnassos.
What to do in Heraklion

Heraklion, the capital of Crete, is located along the north coast of the island. The capital itself has a world to offer holidaymakers, from beaches and bars to shops, sports and sea. The bustling city centre gives way to sumptuous beaches, which can be enjoyed alongside an exploration of Heraklions fascinating culture and history through its many relics and museums.
